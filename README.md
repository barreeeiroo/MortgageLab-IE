# mortgagelab.ie

Irish Mortgage Comparator and Simulator

## Getting Started

Visit [mortgagelab.ie](https://www.mortgagelab.ie) to use the tool.

## Features

### Affordability Calculators

Three calculators aligned with Central Bank of Ireland lending rules:

* **First Time Buyer** - 4x income limit, 90% LTV
* **Home Mover** - 3.5x income limit, 90% LTV, with equity consideration
* **Buy to Let** - 3.5x income limit, 70% LTV

### Rates Comparison

Compare mortgage rates from 10 Irish lenders (AIB, Bank of Ireland, EBS, PTSB,
Avant Money, Haven, ICS Mortgages, MoCo, NÃºa, Credit Union Mortgages). Filter by
LTV, fixed period, buyer type, and BER rating.

### Mortgage Simulator

Model your mortgage with interactive amortization charts. Simulate rate changes,
overpayments, and different scenarios.

## Privacy

* All calculations run locally in your browser
* No data is sent to any server
* No cookies or tracking
* Open source for transparency
